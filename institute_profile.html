Institution's Profile

The main Aim of institution is to provide employment through spinnig, weaving, knitting, stitching, dyeing and making the final products from woollen khadi and polyvastra.

This institution is certified by (KVB) Khadi village industrial board Gujarat. and (KVIC) Khadi & Village Industries Commission of India, Mumbai.

The Organization has contributed immensely to make the people of Ranpur and surrounding villages self-reliant and self-supportive.

The Khadi production is 100% energy free still a fully computerized accounts section is also setup.



Maluba Parmar says that, "I have been working here since I was 18 years old. I earn a living but the atmosphere here and the motivation to work give me a me lot of pleasure. I feel that this organization has made my life not only worth living but also worth enjoying. Trustees and managers are helpful to all of us without any discrimination and that is why we work here wholeheartedly. All the artisans like me are happy with the work and the system that is why we have been sticking here for so long."

Institution's Profile

The institution has provided the residential facility to its employees with just 1 Rupee annual rent for 100 sq. yards of land. Employees of this institution get various benefits and i facilities like M.D.A beneficiary, Artisan Welfare Fund and many other beneficiary schemes from KVIC & KVB.


Total number of person working under this institution are 259.
80% of Ladies
20% of Gents

The administration office is set into prime location of campus. The office consisting of chairman's chamber (Govindsangbhai Dajibhai Dabhi) with meeting facility and Accounting section. Accounting and routine admin work is done with computerized system. Within 23 thousand square yards of land; institute has developed many different divisions and departments for versatile processes of making wool, Khadi and Polyvastra clothes and its products

The campus is full of natural atmosphere which is very nice and pleasing. Here, day starts with prayer. Institution has a special Prayer Hall where all artisans and team meet and start work with prayer.

Let us hear from Shri Govindsangbhai Dabhi who has been the honorary president of Bhal Nalkantha Khadi Gramodyog Mandal, Ranpur Sansthan since 2016, about the wonderful work done by such a Munishri in Bhal region for the development of the whole area.
